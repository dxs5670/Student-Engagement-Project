The organization for the development of Africa at Penn state

ODA-PSU’s mission is to mobilize Penn State students in an effort to initiate action for the social, economic and political development of the African continent.

Tri State

To raise money for pediatric cancer as an independent organization of Penn State Dance Marathon.

Trilogy

Trilogy's mission is to raise money for pediatric cancer through the University's IFC/Panhellenic Dance MaraTHON by yearlong fundraising; as well as perpetuating friendship, unity and involvement among members by engaging in various social activities.

United Nations Children's Fund at Penn State

To organize students for the survival, protection, and development of children worldwide through education, advocacy and fundraising as a U.S. Campus Initiative group through the U.S. Fund for UNICEF.

Volunteer Income Tax Assistance

The purpose of the Penn State Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) program is to provide low income families and individuals with free tax preparation services.

Youth Empowerment Program

The Youth Empowerment Program (YEP) targets vulnerable youth who possess leadership potential but lack opportunities to develop it in a positive way.
